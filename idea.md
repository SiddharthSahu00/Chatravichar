## Domain

Education / Knowledge Management

## User

Students, self-learners, researchers, and technical professionals

## Problem

Students often struggle to convert scattered learning resources such as PDFs, lecture notes, research papers, and online content into structured, concise, and revision-friendly notes. Important concepts, formulas, dependencies, and prerequisites are often missed.

NoteForge is an agentic AI assistant that transforms learning material into organized notes, summaries, revision sheets, concept maps, and self-assessed study plans while ensuring answers remain grounded in the provided content.

## Success Criteria

* Generate high-quality structured notes from source material
* Preserve conceptual accuracy and faithfulness
* Support multi-turn contextual study conversations
* Create revision-focused summaries and quizzes
* Maintain memory across a learning session

## Tool

Local text analysis, outline generation, and quiz generation tools
